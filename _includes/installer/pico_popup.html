1. Enter (UF2) DFU Mode

Press and hold the BOOTSEL button on your Pico while plugging in the USB cable.

2. Ensure DFU Drive Is Mounted

Your computer should detect a new drive (e.g. RPI-RP2). The name may vary depending on your board hardware and bootloader.

3. Download & Copy UF2 to the Drive

Click the button below to download the UF2 file, then copy it to the DFU drive. The device will reboot automatically once the transfer completes and start running AtomVM.
